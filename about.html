Owner Biography

Desmond Trotter


Who?

Desmond Trotter is a second year Computer Information Student at the University of Houston. The 19 year old entered university with over 70 credit hours and currently has over 100 credit hours. Desmond founded The Sweet Spot Cafeteria during his the 2024 Spring Semester in his Web Development class.

Why was The Sweet Spot created?

Desmond created The Sweet Spot for UH students who need a convenient yet filling meal on the Sugar Land campus. The Sweet Spot also serves as hub for students to gather, relax, study, or even network between classes. Desmond's IT and Entrepreneurial background will assist hungry Sugar Land students. His Web Developing skills and Marketing experience will make students aware of The Sweet Spot.

Future Plans?

Once Desmond passes on The Sweet Spot to a new head manager, he plans to work a Data Analyst job after college. While it isn't Desmond's passion to be a Data Analyst, he hopes to explore various avenues within the CIS degree. He hopes to apply his new skills and experience to his family's IT Consulting company in the distant future.
